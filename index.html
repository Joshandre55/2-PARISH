About the Parish

Kayunga parish is under patronage of OUR LADY OF FATIMA, the parish was started by Mill Hill missionaries and was opened on 31.10.1949. The first parish priest was Rev.fr Clemens Joseph Hungo Lampe and the current parish priest is Rev fr Emmanuel Walakira.
